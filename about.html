Our Story

Where we began and where we’re headed

Yazful was founded with one mission — to simplify automation for entrepreneurs and small businesses using real AI tools. Built by Peter Vurgy, an automation strategist with over a decade of hands-on tech experience, Yazful is a response to bloated software stacks and overpriced retainers.

We help service providers automate lead gen, bookings, onboarding, and follow-up using custom systems built on tools like Zapier, GPT-4, and Calendly. No templates. No fluff. Just elite workflows designed to save time and increase revenue.
